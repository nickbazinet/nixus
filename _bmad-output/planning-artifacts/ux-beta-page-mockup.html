What Nixus is — and isn't

Before you download: an honest list so you know if this is worth your time.

  • No bank connection — you upload credit card statements manually (screenshot or PDF).
  • Desktop only — macOS and Windows. No mobile app.
  • Single user — one person's finances per install.
  • AI import requires your own API credentials (stored in your OS keychain).
  • Pre-alpha — features change and things break between releases.
  • Not tax, legal, or investment advice — a tracking tool, not a professional service.

Get started

  1. Download Click the button above for your platform (macOS or Windows).
  2. Install macOS: if Gatekeeper blocks the app, open System Settings → Privacy & Security → Open Anyway. Windows: if SmartScreen appears, click More info → Run anyway. Full install help →
  3. Open Nixus No web signup — your data stays on your machine from the first launch.

Quick questions

Does this connect to my bank?

No, by design. You upload a credit card statement and the AI parses it. Nixus never asks for your bank login.

Where is my data stored?

Locally on your machine. Your budget, accounts, and net worth never leave your computer.

Is it free?

Yes, free for now during pre-alpha. No surprise charges.
